Rainwater Harvesting In Commercial Areas


Rainwater harvesting is old and effective technique of collecting rain water during rainy season for future usage purpose.Water needs in commercial and industrial processes can cost thousands of dollars a year, not only that, but rainwater harvesting helps new construction comply with stormwater regulations in retaining stormwater.

Types of Rainwater Harvesting in Commercial Area:

Surface run-off:

In this process collection, accumulation, treatment or purification, and storing of stormwater for its eventual reuse. It can also include other catchment areas from manmade surfaces, such as roads, or other urban environments .

Roof-Top:

Rooftop water harvesting is a special technique used to conserve water. It is generally used in places where there is lack of water In this technique rainwater is collected on roof and through a pipe it send to a underground tank to store water for further use.

later use of harvested water:

Rainwater harvesting in a public setting can be used for any non-potable water function. This includes:

  • Cooling tower make-up water
  • Toilet Flushes
  • Irrigation
  • Fire Suppression
