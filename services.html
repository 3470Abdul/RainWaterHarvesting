Services We Provide



We offer a range of maintenance, inspection, and testing services that complement our installation systems or help you diagnose issues with your existing water conservation systems. We want to ensure the longevity of your rainwater collection or graywater reuse system and help your protect your investment, no matter if we installed it or another contractor.
  • System Assessment Inspections
  • Individual Maintenance Visits
  • Annual Maintenance Contracts
  • Tank Cleaning
  • Upgrades and Repairs
  • Rainwater Quality Testing

Service, Maintenance, and Inspection Options


SERVICE APPOINTMENT

We provide service appointments for the following systems:

  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • Graywater reuse system
    • If you suspect something is not working correctly, then this service is for you. We will perform a multi-point system inspection, cleaning, and component repair or replacement as necessary.

    SYSTEM ASSESSMENT INSPECTION

    we provide inspections for the following systems

  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • Graywater reuse system
  • Drainage system
    • We will provide a report which documents the system’s current state and what maintenance is required. We can also provide a system demonstration with you

    MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

    If you want your potable rainwater system maintenance performed on auto-pilot, then this annual maintenance service is for you. On each visit, we will perform a multi-point system inspection, starting at the roof, cleaning the various components of the system, and replacing the filters and UV bulb, as required.
